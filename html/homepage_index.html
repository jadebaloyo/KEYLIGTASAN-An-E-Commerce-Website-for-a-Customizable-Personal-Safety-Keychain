About KEYLIGTASAN

Your Trusted Personal Safety Companion

KEYLIGTASAN is an innovative IoT-enabled personal safety keychain designed to protect individuals in crowded and high-risk areas. Our mission is to provide accessible, reliable, and immediate security solutions through cutting-edge technology.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with innovative safety technology, reducing crime rates and enhancing public security in the Philippines.

SDG Alignment

Supporting UN Sustainable Development Goals: SDG 16 (Peace & Justice), SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities), and SDG 9 (Innovation).

Why We Built This

In response to rising theft incidents in crowded places, providing instant emergency alerts and GPS tracking for immediate help.

Why Choose KEYLIGTASAN?

Advanced safety features designed to protect you

Motion Detection

Automatically detects suspicious movements and bag tampering in crowded areas.

SOS Alert System

Instant emergency alerts sent to your contacts and authorities with one touch.

GPS Tracking

Real-time location tracking and sharing for immediate emergency response.

Mobile App Integration

Full control through our user-friendly mobile application for Android.

Long Battery Life

Rechargeable LiPo battery lasts up to 7 days on a single charge.

Bluetooth Connectivity

Seamless connection to your smartphone via Bluetooth Low Energy.
